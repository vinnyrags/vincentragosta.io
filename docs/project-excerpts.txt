PROJECT EXCERPTS
=================
Copy/paste these into the WordPress post_excerpt field for each project.


10glo
-----
A video-first social media platform for the performing arts community — the only product in my portfolio where I built the platform itself, not just the marketing site. React Native mobile app with video recording, editing, and sharing; custom WordPress backend with REST API and ACF-powered content management.

A Beautiful Noise
-----------------
Neil Diamond's biographical jukebox musical — the third catalog-driven show I built after Tina and Jagged Little Pill. The genre was familiar, but the scope was bigger: a custom tour management system spanning 25+ cities, a distributed ticketing model with different providers per venue, and a frontend visual identity that became one of my favorite designs in the portfolio.

ADL
---
My second ADL project through Situation Interactive, following the Never Is Now summit platform. This was the institutional rebuild — a full-site redesign for the Anti-Defamation League's primary digital presence at adl.org, serving the organization's advocacy, education, and public affairs operations across a complex content architecture.

AMA Safety
----------
Brochure website for a workplace safety consulting firm — one of the earliest builds in my independent practice. Custom WordPress theme with service pages, client testimonials, and a contact system built on Gravity Forms.

American Buffalo
----------------
Three Oscar-, Emmy-, and Golden Globe-winning actors in a 16-week limited run — the kind of production where the names sell the tickets and the site needs to stay out of the way. Custom WordPress theme with a star-driven content strategy, ticketing calendar, and structured cast profiles for Laurence Fishburne, Sam Rockwell, and Darren Criss.

Analytic Partners
-----------------
Ground-up site redesign for a global marketing analytics firm — one of two B2B SaaS builds in my Situation portfolio alongside the Criterion platform. Custom WordPress theme with structured case study management, a resource library, and an events system spanning conferences, webinars, and executive briefings.

Anastasia the Musical
---------------------
An inherited codebase rather than a ground-up build — one of a handful of projects in my portfolio where the work was about making meaningful improvements within constraints. Custom WordPress maintenance and feature development for the Broadway production at the Broadhurst Theatre, focused on performance optimization, content updates, and extending an existing platform during the show's run.

Bershtein Law
-------------
Full-stack WordPress build for a New Jersey boutique law firm — one of my earliest direct-client engagements, before the Broadway portfolio existed. Custom theme with practice area architecture, attorney profiles, and a client intake system built on Gravity Forms.

Better Roundabout Theatre
-------------------------
My first engagement with Roundabout Theatre Company — a standalone microsite for the organization's EDI initiative, built at Situation Interactive two years before I would lead the full platform rebuild at roundabouttheatre.org.

Broadway Across America
-----------------------
The largest WordPress Multisite build of my career — a national network spanning 30+ touring venues, each with its own branded subsite, show pages, and local ticketing integrations. Custom theme architecture designed to maintain brand consistency across dozens of markets while giving local teams editorial independence over their venue's content.

Broadway Groups
---------------
The B2B side of Broadway ticketing — a custom WordPress platform for group sales across 50+ productions, with structured show data, availability calendars, and lead capture workflows built for the sales team at Broadway Inbound.

Criterion Ticketing
-------------------
The project where I stepped farthest outside my primary stack — a custom ticketing platform built on Vue.js, Node.js, and PostgreSQL for a multi-venue performing arts organization. Real-time seat selection, dynamic pricing, and a checkout flow processing thousands of transactions weekly.

Dear Evan Hansen
----------------
One of the most design-intensive Broadway builds I worked on — the site that established the ticketing calendar pattern I would reuse across dozens of productions. Custom WordPress theme with a bold visual identity, structured cast profiles, and the first version of the date-driven calendar component that became standard infrastructure at Situation Interactive.

for colored girls

Custom WordPress theme with a ticketing calendar, tour system, and a content architecture that absorbed multiple lead cast changes and a national tour launch without requiring structural modifications.

Rob Lake Ticket Calendar

The Minutes
-----------
A deliberately lean brochure site for Tracy Letts's Pulitzer-finalist play at Studio 54.
