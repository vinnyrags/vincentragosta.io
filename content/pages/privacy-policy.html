Privacy Policy

What this site collects, how it is used, and what choices you have. Last updated March 11, 2026.

Overview

Your privacy matters.

This site is a personal portfolio operated by Vincent Ragosta. It is not an e-commerce platform, does not sell products or services online, and does not run advertising. The information below describes every way this site interacts with your data.

Information collected

Contact form submissions.

This site includes a contact form powered by WPForms. When you submit the form, the information you provide — such as your name, email address, and message — is collected and delivered via email. Form submissions are not stored in a database and are not shared with third parties. Your information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

No analytics or tracking.

This site does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics or tracking service. No behavioral data is collected, no cookies are set for tracking purposes, and no visitor activity is monitored or recorded.

Third-party services

Google Fonts.

This site loads the Fira Code typeface from Google Fonts. When you visit a page, your browser makes a request to Google's servers to retrieve the font files. This request transmits your IP address to Google. Google's privacy policy governs how they handle that data. No other Google services are used on this site.

Yoast SEO.

This site uses the Yoast SEO plugin to generate metadata, XML sitemaps, and structured data for search engines. Yoast operates entirely on the server and does not collect or transmit visitor data to third parties.

Cookies

Minimal cookie usage.

This site does not set tracking cookies or advertising cookies. WordPress may set a small number of functional cookies related to site operation — for example, a cookie to remember your light or dark mode preference. These cookies are not used to identify you or track your behavior across sites.

Your rights

Access, correction, and deletion.

If you have submitted information through the contact form and would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of that data, you can reach me directly at hello@vincentragosta.io. I will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the site's functionality or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
